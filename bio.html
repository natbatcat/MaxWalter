BIO

Max Walter is a Chicago-based comedian originally from Bloomington, Indiana. Max has headlined Zanies Comedy Club in Chicago, where he regularly features for comics such as Mark Normand, Ronny Chieng, Big Jay Oakerson, and many more.

Max won the 2019 Chicago’s Funniest Standup Tournament, the 2016 Funniest Person in Peoria Contest, and the 2019 Fireball Whiskey Mother’s Day “Yo Mama” Joke-Off. He performed in the 2019 Kenan Thompson Ultimate Comedy Experience and was a finalist in the 2018 San Francisco Comedy Competition. Max featured at the legendary Chicago Bronzeville club Jokes and Notes in 2016. His 2014 second-place finish on Wheel of Fortune haunts him to this day.

Max can be seen all around Chicago at clubs like Zanies, Laugh Factory, and Comedy Bar. Follow @maxwaltermax on Instagram to find out where he's preforming next.
